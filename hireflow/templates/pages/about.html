About HireFlow

Streamlining recruitment since 2026

Our Mission

HireFlow is a comprehensive interview scheduling and recruitment management platform built on the robust ERPNext framework. Our mission is to simplify the hiring process for companies of all sizes, from startups to enterprises.

What We Offer

Complete ATS

Track candidates from application to hire with our comprehensive application tracking system.

Smart Scheduling

Automate interview scheduling based on panel availability and candidate preferences.

Multi-round Support

Manage technical, HR, panel, and final interview rounds with ease.

Offer Management

Generate and track offer letters with digital approval workflows.

Built With

HireFlow is built on:

  • Frappe Framework - Full-stack web framework
  • ERPNext v15+ - Enterprise resource planning
  • Python - Backend development
  • MariaDB - Database
